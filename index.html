About Martaucy Publishing

Martaucy Publishing is an independent press committed to bringing meaningful stories to readers of all ages. From literary fiction that challenges and provokes, to children's books that open young minds to the world around them — we believe in the power of words to shape culture.

Founded by MD Taylor, Martaucy Publishing is built on the conviction that independent voices deserve to be heard. Every title we release is chosen with intention, crafted with care, and sent into the world with purpose.
